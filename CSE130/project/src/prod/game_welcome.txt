The Thrall have invaded your homeland and taken your village after a centuries long bloody conflict. 
It has been 80 long days and nights since you have been held captive in this prison. 
Last night you overheard the guards talking about executing all the prisoners since the war is over.
Today is your last chance for escape. Traverse the maze, enter every room, and beat all the guards. Only then will you find a key to free ytour family.

Developers --  Miles Taylor, Harka Katwal, Kenneth Swadley, and Corey Mcclain
